Wordle

How to Play

  • Start typing. The letters will appear in the boxes
  • Remove letters with Backspace
  • Hit Enter/Return to submit an answer
  • Letters with a green background are in the right spot
  • Letters with the yellow background exist in the word, but are in the wrong spots
  • Letters with the grey background do not exist in the word
  • If you need a hint, click the ? icon
